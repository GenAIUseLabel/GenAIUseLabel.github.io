Quick Reference

Stages

  • ID Ideation: choosing a topic, question, or approach.
  • SY Synthesis: understanding, summarizing, or organizing information.
  • AN Analysis: working through evidence, data, code, arguments, or results.
  • DR Drafting: creating text, code, slides, tables, visuals, or other submitted parts.
  • PO Polishing: improving clarity, grammar, tone, formatting, or presentation.

Levels

  • 0-None I did not use GenAI for this stage.
  • 1-Assistive GenAI helped, but I made the substance myself.
  • 2-Collaborative GenAI shaped the structure, reasoning, or content, and I revised it.
  • 3-Generative GenAI produced much of the stage output.
